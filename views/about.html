About Miyawaki Forest

What is Miyawaki Forest?

The Miyawaki method is a unique approach to creating dense, native forests. This technique, developed by Japanese botanist Akira Miyawaki, allows for the creation of forests in a short time span.

Our Mission

At IIT Palakkad, we are committed to creating and maintaining a sustainable ecosystem through the Miyawaki method. The Miyawaki forest in out campus contains about 1200 trees of different species. Our goal is to contribute to environmental conservation while providing a living laboratory for research and education.

Key Features

  • Native species preservation
  • Rapid forest development
  • Biodiversity enhancement
  • Carbon sequestration
  • Educational value
