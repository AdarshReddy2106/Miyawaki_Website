About

The Miyawaki Method is a revolutionary afforestation technique developed by Japanese botanist Dr. Akira Miyawaki, aimed at restoring natural ecosystems by planting dense, native forests. At IIT Palakkad, this method is adopted to create a self-sustaining green space that contributes to biodiversity, improves air quality, and fosters environmental awareness.
