Instructions For Students

To Enter the Data of Miyawaki Project Download the app From Below:

Follow the steps below to successfully use the app:

Step 1: Sign Up

Sign up using your institute email ID.

Create a password for your account. (The password should be at least 6 characters long!)

Step 2: Login 🔓

After signing up, you’ll be redirected to the login page.

Login again using your registered email ID and password.

Step 3: Fill Student Details 🧑‍🎓

Enter your name, roll number, and group name.(These are required to be entered only if you are signing up for the first time.)

Step 4: Enter Tree Data 🌲

Fill in the following tree details:

Tree ID,

Number of primary stems(The number of primary stems should include the main stem and the other primary stems.)

Step 5: Enter Branch Details 🌿

According to the number of primary stems you entered, you’ll be asked to enter the height(m) and circumference(cm) of each stem.

The circumference of the stem should be measured at 13cm from the its origin.

Step 6: Upload Tree Image 📷

Upload or take a photo of the tree.

Ensure a person is holding the levelling staff beside the tree in the image.

Step 7: Save Data 💾

Click Save Data.

You’ll be redirected back to the tree data form to continue entering data for other trees.

Step 8: Editing Data ✏️

Students cannot edit submitted data.

For any changes, please contact your instructor.
